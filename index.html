About Us

We are a private school in the heart of Khayelitsha. We seek to provide quality education that speaks to the African context. We want to produce young Africans that are ready and willing to start their own things in response to African challenges.

  • Quality education is important to us.
  • We inspire the African youth to be tomorrow's leaders.

Benefit to the township

This is an investment in the township to show that even these spaces are worth all the investment we can afford.

Past to the future

We are joining those who have set an example in building our own institutions as African people.
